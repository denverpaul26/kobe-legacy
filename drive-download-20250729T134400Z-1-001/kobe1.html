Nike Kobe 1 Protro (2018)
Release Year: 2018
Design Inspiration: The Kobe 1 Protro is a reimagined version of the original Kobe 1 (released in 2006), blending the classic look with updated performance technologies. The Protro line is all about modernizing Kobe's signature shoes to fit today's game, with improved cushioning, better materials, and a more responsive feel.

Key Features:
- Zoom Air cushioning for responsiveness.
- Sleek, retro-inspired look with modern materials and construction.
- Updated midsole for added comfort and impact protection.

Notable: The Protro version of the Kobe 1 kept the iconic design of the original but offered the performance upgrades needed for modern-day athletes, honoring Kobe’s legacy while keeping up with contemporary basketball footwear standards.
