Nike Kobe V Protro (2020)
Release Year: 2020
Design Inspiration: The Kobe 5 was re-released with enhanced performance features while maintaining the light, low-profile design.

Key Features:
- Updated Zoom Air cushioning for responsiveness.
- Updated materials for better support and comfort.

Notable: The Kobe 5 Protro became one of the most popular retro releases, with colorways like the "Five Rings" edition paying homage to Kobe’s championships.
