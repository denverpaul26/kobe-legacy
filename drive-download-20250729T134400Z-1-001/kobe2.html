Shoes List

Nike Zoom Kobe II (2007)
Release Year: 2007
Design Inspiration: The Kobe II continued the design philosophy of a sleek, fast, and lightweight shoe. This model introduced a more supportive high-top silhouette.

Key Features:
- Full-length Zoom Air cushioning.
- A carbon fiber shank for added stability.
- Premium leather and suede materials.

Notable: The first to feature a more traditional high-top design, adding more ankle support compared to the first model.
