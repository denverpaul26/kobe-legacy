Achievements and Awards

NBA Awards

  • 5× NBA Champion (2000, 2001, 2002, 2009, 2010)
  • NBA MVP 2008
  • 2× NBA Finals MVP (2009, 2010)
  • 18× NBA All-Star (1998, 2000–2016)
  • 4× NBA All-Star Game MVP (2002, 2007, 2009, 2011)
  • 11× All-NBA First Team (2002, 2003, 2004, 2006, 2007, 2008, 2009, 2010, 2011, 2012, 2013)
  • 2× All-NBA Second Team (2000, 2001)
  • 2× All-NBA Third Team (1999, 2005)
  • 9× NBA All-Defensive First Team (2000, 2003, 2004, 2006, 2007, 2008, 2009, 2010, 2011)
  • 3× NBA All-Defensive Second Team (2001, 2002, 2005)
  • 2× NBA Scoring Champion (2006, 2007)
  • NBA Slam Dunk Contest Champion (1997)
  • NBA Most Improved Player (1997)
  • NBA All-Rookie Second Team (1997)

Career Milestones

  • 4th on NBA All-Time Scoring List (33,643 points at retirement)
  • 81-Point Game (2006) – Second-highest single-game performance in NBA history

International Achievements

  • 2× Olympic Gold Medalist (2008, 2012)

Post-Retirement Honors

  • Academy Award for Best Animated Short Film (2018) – *Dear Basketball*
  • Inducted into the Basketball Hall of Fame (2020)
  • Named to the NBA 75th Anniversary Team (2021)

Other Honors

  • Sports Illustrated Sportsman of the Year (2009)
  • Teen Choice Awards – Multiple wins for "Choice Male Athlete"
  • ESPY Awards – Multiple wins

Lakers Franchise Records

  • Numerous franchise records, including points scored, field goals made, and games played
  • Jerseys No. 8 and No. 24 retired by the Los Angeles Lakers
