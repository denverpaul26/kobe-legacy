Nike Zoom Kobe III (2008)
Release Year: 2008
Design Inspiration: A more aggressive design that featured a higher-tech construction to suit Kobe's dynamic game. It incorporated elements from motorsports.

Key Features:
- Carbon fiber midsole for added support.
- Zoom Air cushioning in the forefoot and heel.
- A lightweight, low-profile design.

Notable: TThe shoe featured a sleek, angular design and was considered more futuristic. It received mixed reviews for its bold, non-traditional look.
