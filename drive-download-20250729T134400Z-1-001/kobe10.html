Shoes List

Nike Kobe X (2015)
Release Year: 2015
Design Inspiration: The Kobe X was designed for balance and responsiveness, featuring a more traditional low-top design while maintaining the advanced cushioning and support technologies.

Key Features:
- Zoom Air and Lunarlon cushioning for responsiveness and comfort.
- Flyknit in the upper for a responsive fit.
- Engineered mesh for breathability.

Notable: The shoe’s translucent outsole was a highlight, showcasing the advanced cushioning system. It was also a tribute to Kobe’s ability to adjust his game over the years.
