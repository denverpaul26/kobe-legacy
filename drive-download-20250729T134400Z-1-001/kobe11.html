Nike Kobe XI (2016)
Release Year: 2016
Design Inspiration: The final model in the Kobe signature line, the Kobe XI was designed as a tribute to Kobe’s storied career and emphasized a lightweight, responsive design.

Key Features:
- Flyknit upper for breathability and comfort.
- Lunarlon and Zoom Air cushioning.
- Transparent outsole for a modern look.

Notable: The Kobe XI marked the end of an era for Kobe’s signature line, as it was his last season with Nike before his retirement.
