Nike Kobe VIII Protro (2022)
Release Year: 2022
Design Inspiration: The Kobe 8 Protro is a reimagined version of the original Kobe 8 (released in 2013), known for its lightweight, flexible design. This re-release updated the cushioning and added modern materials to improve overall performance.

Key Features:
- Zoom Air and Lunarlon cushioning for responsiveness and comfort.
- Flyknit-inspired upper for flexibility and breathability.
- Enhanced traction and durability for today’s game.

Notable: The Protro version of the Kobe 8 brought back the lightweight, minimalist design while adding modern technology to elevate its performance. The Kobe 8 has remained a fan-favorite due to its sleek look and fit, and the Protro version honored that legacy with updated features.
